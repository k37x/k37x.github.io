Healthy - Your average health plugin!

A small spigot plugin that gives admins the ability to adjust a player's health, hunger or even add potion effects!
Minecraft version 1.7 to 1.17 supported. Due to the simplicity of this plugin, it may work on future versions.
This site was designed to be lightweight while providing a modern asethetic. Feel free to browse from your Powerbook G4 to your iPhone 12.
